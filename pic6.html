Hyundai Kona New Model

This was such a fun course to produce. This course was a new model course. It was designed to train sales staff about the newest features of a brand new model of Hyundai automobile called Kona. They wanted this course to have lots of eye candy. We used a lot of slow pans, high quality graphics and flash animation along with detailed diagrams, infographics and animations showing the various features and benefits.
